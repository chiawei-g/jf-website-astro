# Where can you attend self-defense classes in Singapore?

*Sep 16, 2019 · Media Feature*

They train ordinary people ranging from kids to adults, with simple yet effective self-defense techniques that aim to end any violent attack in less than 15 seconds.

*JF Self Defense's studio is located at Serangoon Gardens.*

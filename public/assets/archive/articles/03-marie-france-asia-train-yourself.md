# Train Yourself: Place to get self-defense classes in Singapore

*Jul 1, 2019 · Media Feature*

JF Self Defense — specialising in the art of self-defence, JF is committed and passionate about what they do. If you're looking to challenge yourself while having fun in learning to practically guard your safety, you've come to the right place. JF will impart invaluable skills in the department of self-defence, from striking techniques to defence against weapons.

Not all of us are cut out to be Ip Man or Bruce Lee, but **we can all start somewhere**, right? And before you point fingers and look around to see who we're referring to — we're talking about **you**. Although martial arts isn't everyone's cup of tea, it is a key player in the realm of self-defense, which **no one should just brush off as a trivial life skill**. Which of us here is 100% safe from a possible traumatising robbery or a scarring molestation encounter? Being well-versed in the art of self-defense is indubitably one of the most vital skills anyone could arm themselves with. Not only will it level up your safety game and that of the people around you, it's also an extremely impressive party trick. But, of course, the former is more important.

If you're convinced and can't wait to get started, head up to our gallery where we've rounded up 5 of the best places you can get classes from. Don't think you've got what it takes? Unsure if you're strong enough to stomach the training? Well, that's **all the more reason you should start attending these classes**! From striking to shielding techniques, these classes will prepare you for any unexpected encounters with the bad guys. Potential assailants should be praying hard that they don't land you as their next victim!
